Conclusion

Communication, technology and research are all essential components of mechanical engineering. To be successful in this field of work, every aspect of the aforementioned skills has to be honed and mastered. Hopefully, through this paper, I have managed to pave a small pathway to help make it easier for those who are still foreign to the field of mechanical engineering. I believe if a student is equipped with these engineering aptitudes, their career will be one that is fruitful and will no doubt leave a mark not just in the field of engineering, but the world itself.
