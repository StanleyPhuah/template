Anticipated Effects

Nevertheless, I do not deny that life is to be enjoyed. While happiness is relative, to much of this generations youth, when joy comes from binge drinking, partying and experimenting with drugs, is that really true happiness? Once the dust settles and our days are numbered, would they be content with how they lived their life. All it takes is a simple change in perception. Once they fathom what true joy and contentment is, they will understand their purpose, their calling. Thens, only will they truly understand the actual meaning underlying YOLO. Hopefully through education, guidance, and self-realization, the negative culture that is inexplicably tied to YOLO will be transformed to what it truly represents - an expression of hope, a term of encouragement.
